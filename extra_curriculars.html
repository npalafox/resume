My extra curricular activity at the University of Michigan.

I am involved in numerous clubs at Michigan. They can be split into two categories: hobbies and academic interests. I really enjoy all the clubs I am in at Michigan and think they all prepare me to be successful and also bring me joy. My favorite hobby is soccer! I have been playing soccer since I was 8 years old and almost played for a few varsity programs.

Academic Extra Curriculars

MPRODUCT

  • MPRODUCT is the only Product Management club on campus. It is an amazing club that connects me with other students interested in Product Management. I have learned a lot of about customer discovery in this club and in order to be a member I had to go through a 3 week cohort process including multiple tasks that typical Product Managers (PM) do.

Reach Consulting Group

  • I am currently a Project Manager in Reach Consulting Group. Reach Consulting Group is a student-run, non-profit organization providing advisory services in technology, strategy, finance, and management. We are affiliated with the University of Michigan's School of Information and have a strong emphasis on technology consulting.

Athletic Extra Curriculars

Club Soccer (UMMCS)

  • The University of Michigan Men's Club Soccer Team (UMMCS) is the second highlest level of soccer at Michigan. Even though it is club soccer it is a very competitive team that is consistently one of the best in the country.
